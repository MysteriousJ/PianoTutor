All output will appear here, but ensure the Input Capture window is focused
for keyboard inputs to work.
Joystick inputs should work no matter which window has focus.

\Press buttons in the order you want to piano them.
Press enter to confirm the sequence.
You can press backspace to change the sequence at any time.

\Begin practice. The letters show the buttons you press and correct input will
be in alphabetic order.
The numbers show the frames between inputs.
A 0 means the inputs were pressed on the same frame.
After 15 frames with no inputs, it will reset and you can try again.
